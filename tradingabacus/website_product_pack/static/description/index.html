WEBSITE PRODUCT PACK

The module offers you to sell your Odoo product pack on the website. Multiple items can be bundled together to form one product pack. the customer can add the pack only as a whole in the cart containing all the items in it allowing you immense cross-selling and upselling opportunities.

Product Packs to Sell the multiple Products together

When a customer wishes to buy products in bulk then adding such a lot to the cart is really hectic as well as time wasting. If there are packs created for the products then it can reduce the hectic task of customers.

Moreover, you can also add different products to the pack and add the price for the whole pack together. Many times it happens that customers even buy the product which they were not planning to buy because the product is in the pack.

Odoo Website Product Pack facilitates you to create multiple product packs where you can add multiple products or multiple quantities of the same product. Hence, it causes ease to your customers which increases their chance to shop from you again.

Attract Your Customers with Lucrative Price of Product Pack

Odoo Website Product Pack allows you to set the price for the product pack as per your wish. A small discount in the product pack can let your customers buy the whole pack together.

Your Online store will show the product pack price when they choose a pack. Moreover, it will also show the saved amount by them when they select a pack instead of the products individually.

It helps your sellers to boost their sales as they can sell multiple products together as customers can see the benefit clearly. The module offers flexibility to your sellers to manage the packs completely as they want.

Detailed Features List

Below is the detailed list of Feature for Website Product Pack

  • Website Product pack allows to create Bundles of products for websites.
  • Product packs can be differentiated amongst all the products using a ribbon tag.
  • Separate display of all the products available in a product pack.
  • Create a bundle of any of the products related to the Website.
  • Published products of product packs act as a link to the actual products.
  • Product pack is created and published similarly to a product.
  • Display of amount saved on purchase of product pack as compared to buying them individually.
  • In sales order, for picking you have an option to add product pack, products of the product pack or both.
